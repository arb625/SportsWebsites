What is Tennis?

Tennis is a game played with a racquet and a tennis ball. It can be played by two people (singles) or by four people (doubles). The court itself is a rectangular box with a net in the middle of the longer side. The players must keep hitting the ball back to each other over the net into the rectangular box until one person misses a shot and loses the point. Whichever player gets a certain amount of points wins. Tennis can be played both outdoors and indoors and is one of the most popular spectator sports in the world.
